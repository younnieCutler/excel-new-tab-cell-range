CellFocus Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

CellFocus runs as an Office add-in inside Excel and processes workbook content only to display and edit the selected range selected by the user.

Information we collect

CellFocus does not collect personal information, analytics events, telemetry, advertising identifiers, or usage tracking data.

Workbook content

CellFocus reads workbook cell values and formatting from the active Excel session so it can render the selected range in the task pane. When the user edits a cell in CellFocus, the edited value is written back to the workbook through the Office JavaScript API.

CellFocus does not operate a separate application backend for workbook contents and does not intentionally transmit workbook contents to a CellFocus server.

Hosting and Microsoft services

CellFocus loads its static add-in files over HTTPS from GitHub Pages. The add-in also uses Microsoft Office Add-ins platform services and the Office JavaScript API provided by Microsoft. Your use of Excel, Microsoft 365, SharePoint, OneDrive, and Office Add-ins remains subject to Microsoft's terms and privacy practices.

Data retention

CellFocus does not maintain a server-side database and does not store workbook contents on a CellFocus-operated backend. Browser or Office WebView storage may be used locally by the add-in runtime for task pane state such as open tabs.

Third-party sharing

CellFocus does not sell personal information and does not share workbook contents with advertisers or analytics providers.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at https://github.com/younnieCutler/excel-new-tab-cell-range/issues.
