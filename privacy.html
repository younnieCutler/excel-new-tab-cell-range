CellFocus Privacy Policy

CellFocus runs as an Office add-in inside Excel and processes workbook content only to display and edit the selected range.

Data collection

CellFocus does not include analytics, telemetry, advertising trackers, or a backend service in this build.

Workbook data

Workbook cell values and formatting are read from the active Excel session and written back to the workbook when the user edits cells in CellFocus.

External services

CellFocus loads its static add-in files over HTTPS. It does not send workbook contents to a separate application server.
