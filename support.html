CellFocus Support

CellFocus is an Excel add-in for focused editing of selected cell ranges in a task pane.

Getting help

If CellFocus does not load, confirm that your Excel client can access this site over HTTPS and that Office add-ins are enabled by your organization.

Common checks

  • Use Microsoft Excel, then open your workbook normally.
  • Select a range, then use the CellFocus ribbon button or context menu command.
  • If the task pane is stale, close and reopen the CellFocus pane.
